Our Services

Valley Catering Co handles events of all sizes, from intimate gatherings of 20 to large corporate functions of 500+. Our team manages setup, service, and cleanup so you can focus on your guests.

  • Corporate lunches and dinners
  • Wedding receptions and rehearsal dinners
  • Private parties and milestone celebrations
  • Boxed lunch and drop-off catering
  • Buffet and plated service available
  • Dietary accommodations: vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free
